Specialized Gold Processing

Advanced gold extraction technologies designed for maximum recovery and environmental sustainability

Vat Leaching of Gold Ores

Our vat leaching technology provides efficient gold extraction from low-grade ores through controlled cyanide solution percolation. This method offers excellent recovery rates while maintaining cost-effectiveness for medium-scale operations.

High gold recovery rates (85-95%)
Controlled leaching environment
Suitable for low to medium grade ores
Reduced reagent consumption

Heap Leaching of Gold Ores

Our heap leaching solutions enable large-scale gold extraction from low-grade ores through engineered heap construction and solution management. This technology maximizes resource utilization while minimizing environmental impact.

Large-scale processing capability
Cost-effective for low-grade ores
Environmental compliance systems
Extended leaching cycles
